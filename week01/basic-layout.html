Cordoba- Argentina

Córdoba is known for its Spanish colonial architecture. It is home to the Jesuit Block, a 17th-century complex with active cloisters, churches and the original campus of the National University of Córdoba, one of the oldest universities in South America. The central point of the city is the Plaza San Martín and the neo-baroque style Córdoba Cathedral It is a very diverse city, with a small metropolis, rivers, lakes, mountains and countryside. an excellent option for vacation
